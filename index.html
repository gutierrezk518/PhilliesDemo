WAR Leaderboard

Wins Above Replacement estimates how many extra wins a player gave the Phillies over a freely-available replacement-level player (think: a minor-league call-up or waiver-wire pickup). It rolls hitting, baserunning, fielding, and pitching into a single number — so a star shortstop and an ace starter can be compared on the same scale. As a rough guide: 2 WAR is a solid starter, 5 WAR is an All-Star, and 8+ WAR is an MVP-caliber season.

All WAR values on this page use bWAR, sourced from Baseball-Reference's Phillies franchise leaders (through the 2025 season).
